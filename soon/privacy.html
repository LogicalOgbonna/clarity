Clarity Privacy

Your privacy is our default setting

Clarity collects just the basics needed to personalize your experience and provide premium features. We do not track, profile, or monetize your data.

We only ask for information you choose to share with us:

Name Email Credit card (premium only)

How we use your info

  • Name lets us personalize your dashboard and communications.
  • Email helps us create your account, send updates you opt into, and assist with support.
  • Credit card details are processed securely for premium billing and never stored directly by us.

What we never do

  • No cookies or trackers. We don’t store, read, or process browser cookies.
  • No third-party advertising. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared without your explicit consent.
  • No surprise collection. If we don’t ask for it here, we don’t collect it anywhere else.

Your choices, protected

  • You can access, update, or delete your personal details at any time inside Clarity.
  • Premium billing runs through trusted payment partners that meet top security standards.
  • We review our privacy posture regularly and update this notice whenever things change.
Need help or have a privacy question?

Email our privacy team at privacy@clarity.click and we’ll respond promptly.
